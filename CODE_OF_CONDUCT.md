## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### ‚úÖ Expected Behavior
- **Respectful Communication**: Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions
- **Constructive Feedback**: Provide feedback that helps improve code and research
- **Scientific Integrity**: Maintain high standards of research ethics and reproducibility
- **Collaborative Spirit**: Help others learn and contribute effectively
- **Professional Conduct**: Maintain professional standards in all discussions

### ‚ùå Unacceptable Behavior
- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- **Disrespectful Language**: Use of derogatory, discriminatory, or inflammatory language
- **Personal Attacks**: Attacking individuals rather than ideas or code
- **Spam**: Excessive self-promotion or off-topic content
- **Data Misuse**: Unauthorized use or sharing of sensitive data
- **Research Misconduct**: Plagiarism, data fabrication, or unethical research practices

## Research Ethics

As a research project focused on AI in finance, we have additional ethical standards:

### üìä Data Integrity
- **Honest Reporting**: Report results accurately, including negative findings
- **Data Privacy**: Protect sensitive financial data and personal information
- **Transparency**: Document methodologies, assumptions, and limitations
- **Reproducibility**: Enable others to reproduce and verify results

### ü§ñ AI Ethics
- **Responsible AI**: Consider broader implications of AI in financial systems
- **Bias Awareness**: Actively work to identify and mitigate algorithmic biases
- **Open Science**: Share knowledge and tools to advance the field
- **Public Benefit**: Focus on research that benefits society

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:
1. **GitHub Issue**: Create a private issue with "Code of Conduct Violation" in the title
2. **Direct Contact**: Email the maintainers privately
3. **Anonymous Reporting**: Use anonymous reporting channels if preferred

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

### Investigation Process
1. **Acknowledgment**: Reports will be acknowledged within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: Incidents will be investigated promptly and fairly
3. **Resolution**: Appropriate action will be taken based on findings
4. **Communication**: Resolution will be communicated to involved parties
5. **Follow-up**: Measures to prevent recurrence will be implemented

### Enforcement Actions
Depending on severity, violations may result in:
- **Warning**: Verbal or written warning with clear expectations
- **Temporary Ban**: Suspension from community spaces for a specified period
- **Permanent Ban**: Removal from community with no possibility of return
- **Legal Action**: Involvement of authorities for severe violations
